    [Spring 2019] Isekai Quartet Discussion Thread

    04-09-2019, 10:37 AM
    Widgets Magazine
    異世界かるてっと

    A short about characters from four different Kadokawa isekai shows have gathered into one place. As such, characters from Konosuba, Re:Zero, Overlord, and Saga of Tanya the Evil are interacting with each other as this isn't the first time they have been isekaied.

    Airs: Tuesday, April 9, 2019

    Studio: PuYUKAI

    ANN Entry
    My Anime List
    Official Site
    Funimation

    A mysterious switch appeared one day. Upon pressing it, they were sent to a different alternative world!! There are also characters from other alternative worlds gathered together...!?

    Episode 1

    Four-in-one.

    And the characters from the four Kadokawa isekai shows have gathered into one place at Isekai High School after pushing the red button in different ways. The circumstances in which that happened is quite funny, and now the main cast from Konosuba, Overlord, Saga of Tanya the Evil, and Re:Zero have gathered under one roof. That was pretty amusing to watch these characters finding themselves talking to each other as everybody got isekaied, and for the four leads of the shows its happening again. Seeing that this is the same studio that did the Re:Zero shorts that this is going to be fun.

                      Episode 5

                      Taking their talents to another world.

                      It's time for the talent show, and some people just don't have any talents, especially Darkness there as all she wants is to show just how much endurance she has after taking a beating. But then Megumin shows just how much firepower she has in her explosions and the class is impressed. Just when they thought they had seen it all, they haven't seen what Ains can do as he turns the place into a winter wonderland with his magic. Once again, it's amusing to see all of these characters interacting with each other, each with a special talent that may be nothing more than useless party tricks or something that isn't exactly useful in combat.
